The Salesforce Integration enables your GenAI application to authenticate users with their Salesforce accounts and access CRM data through Token Vault.

## Overview

Salesforce is the world's leading CRM platform. This integration allows AI agents to access customer data, manage sales processes, and automate business workflows within the Salesforce ecosystem.

## Configuration

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Connected App">
    1. In Salesforce Setup, go to **App Manager**
    2. Click **New Connected App**
    3. Enable OAuth settings and configure scopes
    4. Note your **Consumer Key** and **Consumer Secret**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up Auth0 Integration">
    1. In Auth0 Dashboard, go to **Authentication** > **Enterprise**
    2. Select **OIDC** and configure for Salesforce
    3. Enable **Token Vault** in Advanced settings
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Required Scopes

| Scope | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| `api` | Access and manage data |
| `refresh_token` | Maintain access |
| `openid` | OpenID Connect authentication |
| `profile` | Access user profile |
