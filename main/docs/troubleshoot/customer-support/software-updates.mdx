During the subscription term, Auth0 will provide or install updates if and when they are made generally commercially available by Auth0 to its customers. There is no additional cost to you.

For pricing information on Auth0 subscription plans, read the [Pricing Page](https://auth0.com/pricing) or go to your [Tenant Settings](https://manage.auth0.com/#/tenant/billing/subscription).
